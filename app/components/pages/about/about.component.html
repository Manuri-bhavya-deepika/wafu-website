Working Process

Simple & Clean Work Process

  • Frame the Problem 1

    Understanding the problem clearly before finding solutions. This involves identifying important factors, limitations, and goals to make better decisions.

  • Collect the Raw Data 2

    Gathering all the basic information related to the problem. This helps to understand the situation better and form a solid foundation for finding solutions

  • Process the Data 3

    Organizing and analyzing the gathered information to make it useful. This helps to identify patterns and insights that guide decision-making.

  • 4Explore the Data

    Examining the organized data to uncover trends, patterns, and insights. This helps to understand the key factors that impact the problem.

  • 5Perform Analysis

    Examining the data in detail to draw conclusions. This helps to identify possible causes, solutions, and make informed decisions.

  • 6Communicate Results

    Sharing the findings in a clear and understandable way. This helps others grasp the insights and make decisions based on the analysis.
